ACM at UK is an experience. We strive to foster a strong community for computing students at the University of Kentucky. We're most famous for our annual hackathon, CatHacks, but we also put on several other events throughout the year.

UK ACM meetings occur each Thursday from 6:00PM-7:00PM and are open to all UK students. You can find us in the Engineering Annex.
